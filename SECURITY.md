## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security and safety of the Bangkong project seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability or a potential safety risk, please report it responsibly.

### How to Report

**Do not open a public issue.**

Please email us directly at **bilbobangkong@gmail.com** with the following details:
1.  **Description:** A clear description of the vulnerability or risk.
2.  **Steps to Reproduce:** How we can see the issue ourselves.
3.  **Impact:** What an attacker or bad actor could do with this information.
4.  **Suggested Fix (Optional):** If you have a solution, please share it.

### What to Expect

-   **Acknowledgment:** We will confirm receipt of your report within **48 hours**.
-   **Investigation:** We will investigate the issue and determine the severity.
-   **Resolution:** We will work on a fix and notify you once it is resolved.
-   **Disclosure:** We ask that you keep the issue private until we have released a patch or mitigation.

### AI Safety & Misuse

Since Bangkong is an AI training system, we also consider **potential for misuse** as a safety concern. If you find a way the code could be easily weaponized or used for harmful purposes (e.g., generating malware, deepfakes, or spam), please report it via the same email. We are committed to building responsible AI tools.

### Safe Harbor

We support safe harbor for security researchers who report vulnerabilities in good faith. We will not take legal action against researchers who follow this policy.

Thank you for helping keep Bangkong safe!
